MY INTERESTS

I’m someone driven by curiosity not limited to one path, but constantly exploring across different fields. My interests span a wide range, from coding and problem-solving to building small IoT projects, and even extending into creative areas like drawing, painting, and design. For me, it's not just about learning skills, but about using them to build meaningful and aesthetically thoughtful things. I believe in continuous self-improvement. Every opportunity to learn something new — no matter how small — is something I value. Over time, this mindset has helped me become more adaptable, creative, and disciplined.
