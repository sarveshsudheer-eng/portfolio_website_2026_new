I HAVE ALSO DONE ONE OF IOT PROJECTS THAT IS FOR PARKINSON PEOPLE , IT IS A SPPOON THAT SELF STABILIZES SO THAT THEY CAN HAVE THE FOOD PROPERLY

self-stabilizing spoon is a smart utensil designed to help people with hand tremors (such as those caused by Parkinson’s disease) eat more easily. It uses sensors and small motors to detect unwanted shaking and automatically counteract those movements in real time. So even if the user’s hand is shaking, the spoon’s bowl stays relatively steady, reducing spills and making eating more comfortable and independent.
